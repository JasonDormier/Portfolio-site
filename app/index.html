UI/UX

Code

Problem Solving

Design Process

I have a proven design process that has had it’s metal tested across multiple industries of design. I will identify what are the fundamental building blocks of your project, research, prototype, design, and iterate keeping you in loop the whole time. My design process includes you, and I want your experience to be as great as the end product we develop together.

Human Centered Design

Understanding the needs of my clients, means understanding the people they are trying to communicate with and that is where HCD starts.

Iteration

We will iterate through the design together to make sure we are telling a story that is true to the needs of your business, and communicates the message in a way to conveys empathy to your customers.

End Product
